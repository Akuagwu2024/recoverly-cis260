About Recoverly

Built by students, for CCC community — Recoverly is a practical solution to a common campus problem. Whether you’ve lost something or found something, this platform helps you take action quickly.

Our Culture

At every City College, lost items are treated with care — recovery is woven into our culture of respect and responsibility.

Vision

A campus where every lost item is met with empathy, and every recovery strengthens trust.

Mission

Built by students to solve real problems — Recoverly honors the dignity of return, connecting our community through honesty, care, and shared responsibility.
